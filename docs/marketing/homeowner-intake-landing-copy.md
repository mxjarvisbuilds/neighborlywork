## FAQ

### How does NeighborlyWork work for homeowners?
You answer a short HVAC intake form with your project type, home details, current system, urgency, and contact information. NeighborlyWork uses that request to match you with up to 3 vetted local contractors who can submit quotes.

### Is it free to request quotes?
Yes. The homeowner quote request is free, and you are not required to choose a contractor.

### What areas do you serve?
NeighborlyWork is starting with Sacramento-area homeowners, including Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Elk Grove, and nearby communities as contractor coverage allows.

### Will contractors see each other's prices?
No. Contractors submit blind quotes. That means each contractor gives their own price and scope without seeing competing bids first.

### What happens after I choose a quote?
After you choose the contractor you want to move forward with, NeighborlyWork updates your request and helps keep the project status organized. The contractor handles the install details directly with you.
