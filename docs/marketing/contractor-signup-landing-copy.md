# Contractor Signup Landing Page Copy

## Page goal
Get qualified Sacramento-area HVAC contractors to create a NeighborlyWork contractor account and agree to the launch referral terms.

## Primary CTA
Apply to Receive HVAC Leads

## Hero value proposition

### Headline
Win HVAC install jobs without buying junk leads.

### Subhead
NeighborlyWork sends qualified homeowner quote requests to vetted local contractors. No monthly subscription, no upfront lead-buying, and no fee unless you win and complete the install.

### Supporting bullets
- Homeowners request HVAC quotes before you ever engage.
- Up to 3 vetted contractors compete in a blind quote process.
- You submit your own price, equipment, warranty, and timeline.
- You pay only when a matched install closes.

## How it works

### 1. Apply and verify your business
Create a contractor account, share your company details, and confirm service area, license, insurance, and HVAC specialties.

### 2. Receive matched homeowner requests
NeighborlyWork matches eligible HVAC requests to up to 3 contractors based on location, fit, and availability.

### 3. Submit a blind quote
Review the project details and submit your quote without seeing competitor pricing. Include equipment, warranty, timeline, scope notes, and total price.

### 4. Homeowner compares options
The homeowner compares quotes side by side and chooses the contractor they want to move forward with.

### 5. Complete the install and pay on close
If the homeowner selects you and the install is completed, the closed-install referral fee becomes owed under the launch terms.

## Payment terms

### Launch referral fee
$800 per closed HVAC install or replacement job.

### When the fee is owed
The fee is owed only when all of the following are true:
- The lead came through NeighborlyWork.
- The homeowner selected your company.
- The job became a completed HVAC install or replacement.

### When no fee is owed
No fee is owed for:
- Lost bids.
- Unselected quotes.
- Leads that never become completed installs.
- Homeowner cancellations before completion.
- Monthly access or subscription charges.

### Billing timing
The launch fee is billed after install completion under the active contractor agreement and billing workflow. Current signup copy states the fee is due within 30 days after the completed install.

## FAQ

### Do I pay for every lead?
No. NeighborlyWork does not charge for every lead or every quote. The launch fee applies only when you win and complete a matched HVAC install or replacement.

### Are quotes exclusive?
No. A homeowner request may be matched with up to 3 vetted contractors. The process is blind, so contractors do not see each other's bids.

### Can I choose which requests to quote?
Yes. You can review matched request details and decide whether to submit a quote based on your service area, schedule, project type, and fit.

### What information should my quote include?
A strong quote should include total price, equipment details, warranty, estimated timeline, included scope, and any assumptions or exclusions the homeowner should understand.

### What if the homeowner cancels or the job does not close?
No closed-install fee is owed if the homeowner cancels before completion or the project does not become a completed install.

### Is there a monthly fee?
No. The launch contractor model has no monthly subscription or recurring listing fee.
