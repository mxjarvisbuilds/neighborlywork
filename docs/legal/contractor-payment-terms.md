# NeighborlyWork Contractor Payment Terms

Launch terms for contractor-facing copy, legal review, and PDF/export use.

- No monthly fee.
- No subscription fee.
- No pay-per-lead fee.
- Launch referral fee: **$800 per closed HVAC install or replacement job**.
- Fee is owed only when a NeighborlyWork-matched homeowner selects the contractor and the install/replacement is completed.
- No fee is owed for lost bids, unselected quotes, homeowner cancellations before completion, service-only calls that do not become installs, or jobs that do not close.
- Contractors may be required to authorize a Stripe payment method before receiving/continuing to receive matched leads.
- Stripe authorization does not create an immediate charge.
- Stripe billing may be used to collect the $800 referral fee after a qualifying completed install creates a billing cycle.
- Stripe remains **test-mode only** until Zayith explicitly approves live mode.
- Contractors must maintain required license/insurance, quote honestly, comply with laws/codes, and avoid fee circumvention.

Canonical public surfaces:
- `/terms.html`
- `/contractor-agreement.html`
- Contractor signup agreement step in `/app/contractor-auth.html`
- Fee Terms tab in `/app/contractor-portal.html`
