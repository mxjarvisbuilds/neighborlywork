NeighborlyWork Contractor Agreement

Launch version — effective April 25, 2026

This Contractor Agreement summarizes the contractor-specific terms for approved HVAC contractors using NeighborlyWork. It works together with the NeighborlyWork Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Core payment terms: no monthly fee, no subscription fee, no pay-per-lead fee. The launch referral fee is $800 per closed HVAC install or replacement job that originated through NeighborlyWork.

1. Marketplace relationship

NeighborlyWork routes homeowner HVAC quote requests to vetted local contractors. Contractors are independent businesses, not employees, agents, or franchisees of NeighborlyWork.

2. Lead routing and blind bidding

NeighborlyWork may route a homeowner request to up to three vetted contractors based on service area, project fit, availability, profile completeness, and platform quality signals. Contractors submit blind quotes and do not see competing contractor pricing before submitting their own quote.

3. Contractor obligations

Contractors agree to maintain required licenses and insurance, provide accurate company/profile information, quote honestly, honor submitted quote terms, communicate professionally, comply with applicable laws and building codes, and avoid circumventing NeighborlyWork fees for matched homeowners.

4. Referral fee

The contractor owes NeighborlyWork $800 only when a NeighborlyWork-matched homeowner selects the contractor and the HVAC install or replacement is completed. No fee is owed for lost bids, unselected quotes, homeowner cancellations before completion, service/diagnostic visits that do not become installs, or jobs that do not close.

5. Stripe payment authorization

Contractors may be required to authorize a Stripe payment method before receiving or continuing to receive matched leads. Authorization does not create an immediate charge. NeighborlyWork may use the authorized Stripe billing profile to collect the $800 referral fee after a qualifying completed install creates a billing cycle. Until explicitly approved for live launch, Stripe flows remain in test mode.

6. Billing timing and disputes

The referral fee is billed after completed installation and is due within 30 days unless a different written payment schedule is agreed. Contractors should dispute billing issues promptly with supporting details. NeighborlyWork may suspend lead routing for unpaid, failed, or disputed billing profiles.

7. No exclusivity or guarantee

NeighborlyWork does not guarantee lead volume, quote selection, job close rate, revenue, or exclusive territory. Homeowners choose contractors independently.

8. Acknowledgment

By signing up, checking the contractor agreement box, authorizing billing, or using the contractor portal, the contractor acknowledges these terms and the Terms of Service.
