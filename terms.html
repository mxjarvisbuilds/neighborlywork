4. How the Platform Works

For Homeowners: Homeowners submit their HVAC project details through our intake form. NeighborlyWork matches the homeowner with up to three vetted local contractors who serve their area. Each contractor submits a blind quote — contractors never see each other's pricing. The homeowner reviews and compares the quotes and selects the contractor of their choice. The homeowner then works directly with the selected contractor for scheduling, installation, and payment for the HVAC work itself.

For Contractors: Contractors apply to join the NeighborlyWork network and are subject to approval. Approved contractors receive matched leads based on their service area and specialties. Contractors submit blind quotes for each lead. If a homeowner selects a contractor and the job is completed and installed, the contractor pays NeighborlyWork the contractor referral fee described below.

Contractor Referral Fee: $800 flat fee per closed install job.

When it is owed: Only when (a) the homeowner selects the contractor through NeighborlyWork and (b) the matched HVAC install or replacement is completed.

What counts as a closed job: A NeighborlyWork-matched homeowner project that becomes a completed HVAC install or replacement performed by the selected contractor.

Billing timing: The fee is billed after the completed install and is due within 30 days.

If a job falls through: No fee is owed for lost bids, unselected quotes, homeowner cancellations before completion, or jobs that do not reach completed installation.

No recurring charges: There are no monthly fees, subscription fees, or charges for leads that do not become closed install jobs.
